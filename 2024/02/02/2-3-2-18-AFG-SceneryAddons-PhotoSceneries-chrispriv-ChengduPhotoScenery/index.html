Chengdu Photo Scenery
OrganicCabbage
  2024-02-02 00:00:00   2026-01-05 11:50:07  211 Words

[Scenery Addons]

Photo scenery in HD covering the town of Chengdu and its surroundings, incl. the mountains in the west. An elevation fix was made especially for the airport area.

Works together with AFG with global data streaming (images also may be used with FS2023).

images: contains the image files

File: dlc_scenery_cn_chengdu_images.tme

fsg: contains elevation data

File: dlc_scenery_cn_chengdu_fsg.tme

[Coverage]

[Preview]

[Installation]

Move the “.tme” files to the following path:

FS Global: …/storage/emulated/0/Android/data/com.aerofly.aeroflyfsg1/files/

Having problems? Please visit the FAQs.

[References]

Photo-texture based on ArcGIS map © with a resolution of 4.777 meters/pixels and higher resolution coverage for the town and airport areas with 1.194 meters/pixels.

Elevation Data from OpenTopography © using Copernicus Global 30m data.
